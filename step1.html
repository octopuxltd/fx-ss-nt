To hide the real Firefox toolbar:

  1. Open a new tab and navigate to about:config
  2. Click ‘Accept the risk and continue’
  3. Paste this into the text input: full-screen-api.ignore-widgets
  4. Click the icon on the right to set that pref to ‘true’
  5. Close that tab
  6. Press F11 to toggle the toolbar on and off. If that does nothing, try Fn+F11 (common on laptops). Press Ctrl+Shift+F to toggle the toolbar on and off.
  7. This has remapped Firefox’s ‘full screen’ control to be ‘hide toolbar’ – remember to set it back to false later to restore the standard full screen functionality.
